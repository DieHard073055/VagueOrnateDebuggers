Court of Humor

Order in the Court – Where Laughter Reigns Supreme!

Section 69.420

In the jurisdiction of joy, all are required to partake in the pursuit of happiness and the practice of hearty laughter.

The Consequences

By the power vested in the spirits of humor, the accused are hereby sentenced to a delightful day of joy, to be spent in the company of the jester who has brought forth this charge. Let the good times roll!
